About Kinga Sek

Hi! My name is Kinga, I'm from Poland, and I've been living in London for the past 8 years.
Travelling is one of my greatest passions, which is why I got my bachelor's degree in Tourism Management.

However, during my studies, I realized how wrong I was about the chosen direction. While I was looking for an alternative route,
I came across an e-mail from Code First Girls that I found in my university inbox. I took a deeper look into it, I was extremely impressed
with CFG mission and free tech courses. I decided to apply for one of the @uni kickstarters courses. After finishing the Python&App course,
I was delighted to notice how much fun coding gives me. To broaden my limited knowledge, I decided to sign up for the Web Dev course, and therefore, here I am!

I want encourage every woman who is wondering about a carrer in tech to give it a go!
CFG is a fantastic place to start! Coding is much more fun than it seems at first glance!
